🌍 DoloSil-20 · SAI Trilemma Screening — Figure & Data Repository

Project Title: Resolving the SAI Trilemma with a Novel Core–Shell Mineral Aerosol: DoloSil-20, a Silica-Passivated Dolomite Architecture for Simultaneous Optical Efficiency, Thermal Neutrality, and Ozone Safety

👤 Abdul Haseeb Tanoli 👤 Shams ul Arfeen 🏛️ PIEAS, Islamabad 📄 EarthArXiv 🎯 Atmospheric Chemistry and Physics (ACP)

This repository contains the principal figures and supporting visual PDFs associated with the DoloSil-20 study on Stratospheric Aerosol Injection (SAI) and the atmospheric engineering “SAI trilemma”.

📖 Overview

The repository is organized to support manuscript preparation, peer review, figure archiving, and scientific transparency.

Core goals

  • High optical efficiency
  • Thermal neutrality
  • Ozone safety
